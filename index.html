About Me

Academic Leader,
Educator & Researcher

I am currently serving as the Head of the Department of Computer Engineering & Applications (CEA), the largest department of the university, with more than 12,000 students and over 200 learned faculty members. As the Head of the Department, I have taken several strategic initiatives to strengthen placement opportunities and create a placement-oriented academic environment.

Under my leadership, students secured placements in reputed organizations such as ServiceNow, Autodesk, JPMorgan, FourKites, Philips, Siemens Healthineers, NI Systems, HWI, Capgemini, Accenture, and Cognizant. I introduced the “TBPPP” (Task-Based Pre-Placement Preparation) initiative and launched industry-oriented technical tracks from the second year onwards to align students’ skills with industry requirements. Additionally, I formed a five-member task force to ensure discipline, effective class conduction, and academic monitoring across all blocks. I also implemented innovative academic models such as “4+2” in the final year and “3+3” in the third year to enhance placement preparation alongside academics. As a result of these initiatives, the department achieved remarkable placement outcomes this year, including 312 selections in Capgemini, 242 in Cognizant, 170 in Accenture, 392 students shortlisted in the first round of HWI, along with selections in NI Systems, Microsoft, Amazon, and several other reputed organizations
